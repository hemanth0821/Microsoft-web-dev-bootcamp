Nelson Mandela

Born in a small village named Mvezo in South Africa, Nelson Mandela was originally named as Rolihlahla Mandela and grew up listening to the stories of valour of his ancestors during the wars of resistance. In the early 1940s, he chose to study a law degree at the Witswaterand University where he got to know people from all races and backgrounds.

Coco Chanel

Gabrielle Bonheur “Coco” Chanel, a French fashion designer and a businesswoman who established the prolific fashion brand Chanel. From little black dress, tweed jackets, bell-bottomed pants, gold-chained handbags, menswear and womenswear to signature scent, Coco Chanel has contributed to the sphere of fashion and designing making her one of the great personalities.
